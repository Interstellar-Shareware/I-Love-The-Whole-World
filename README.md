## I Love The Whole World, It's Such a Brilliant Place

I Love The Whole World is a celebration of the interconnectedness of humanity.

Clicking on a page title reveals all pages that link away from that page on Wikipedia, creating a branching tree of all of human knowledge.

World leads to Phosphine which leads to Psychedelic Rock which leads to Cessna 172 which leads to...

See what you can find.

Made with love and a book and the JS docs and no AI because people are cool and the current state of AI utilization and culture sucks.
